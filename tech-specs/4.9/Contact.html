Business Object: Contact

Definition

Contacts are people who may be associated directly with CRM accounts, customers, ship-to addresses, vendors, vendor addresses, and sites.

Related Information

    Other

  • The Contact has no cache defined
  • Contacts are lockable.
  • Used in the crm and project module
  • Contacts can be read by users with "ViewAllContacts" privilege and can be created, updated, or deleted by users with the "MaintainAllContacts" privilege.
  • Users with "ViewPersonalContacts" privilege can read their personal contacts but cannot read the contact's owned by other users. Users with "MaintainPersonalContacts" privilege can create, update or delete their personal contacts but not the contacts owned by other users
  • Comments panel should exist to add new comments to the Contact
  • Documents panel should exist to connect the Contacts to : Account, contact, Customer, File, Incident, Item, Link, Opportunity, Project, To do
  • Email Addresses panel should exist to add email addresses related to the contact
  • TO DO panel should exist to attach existing/new To Dos to the Contact
  • Opportunities panel should exist to attach existing/new Opportunities to the Contact
  • Incidents panel should exist to attach existing/new Incidents to the Contact
  • Projects panel should exist to attach existing/new Projects to the Contact
  • Contacts attached as a primary/secondary contact to a CRM account cannot be deleted
