Business Object: Customer

Definition

The "Customers" screen lists all your company's Customers. In addition, the screen can be used to generate Customer reports(for example, Customers by Customer Type)

Related Information

    Other

  • The customer collection is not cached.
  • Customer is lockable.
  • The ID attribute is "number", which will be automatically uppercased.
  • Used in the Billing and Sales modules
  • Users can create, update, and delete Customers if they have the 'MaintainCustomerMasters' privilege, and they can read Customers if they have the 'ViewCustomerMasters' privilege.
  • Comments panel should exist to add new comments to the Customer
  • Documents panel should exist to connect the Customers to : Account, contact, Customer, File, Incident, Item, Link, Opportunity, Project, To do
  • Quotes panel should exist to attach existing/new Quotes to the Customer
  • Sales Orders panel should exist to attach existing/new SalesOrders to the Customer
  • Contacts panel should exist to attach existing/new contacts to the Customer
  • TO DO panel should exist to attach existing/new To Dos to the Customer
  • Opportunities panel should exist to attach existing/new Opportunities to the Customer
  • Incidents panel should exist to attach existing/new Incidents to the Customer
  • Credit Cards panel should exist to create/edit Credit Cards
  • Delete option should be disabled for the Customers with sales history
  • Used in the Billing and Sales modules

    CustomerShipTo

  • Select to display Ship-To Addresses for the specified Customer. Every Customer can have multiple Ship-To Addresses, one for each of the destinations you ship goods to. Ship-To Addresses are specified at the point of Sales Order entry
