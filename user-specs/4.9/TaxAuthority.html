Related Information

    Other

  • The Tax Authority collection is cached.
  • TaxAuthority is not lockable.
  • The ID attribute is "code", which will be automatically uppercased.
  • Users can create, update, and delete TaxAuthorities if they have the 'MaintainTaxAuthorities' privilege, and they can read TaxAuthorities if they have the 'ViewTaxAuthorities' privilege.
  • Delete option should be disabled for the Tax Authorities which are assigned to Tax Codes
